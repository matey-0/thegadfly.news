About The Gadfly

The Gadfly is the worst (and only) satire newspaper at LHSS. Before you stop reading, just know that we also hate the idea. The reason we're special is because we publish lies (see: satire). If you're a grefegenkalent admin, just know that we're super official; we have a constitution that in no way satirises you!

Our History

The Beginning - [May 2025]

In the beginning, there was nothing. A few billion years later, The Backcast was created. A satirical paper similar to The Gadfly, (but worse in every way). it's almost laughable looking back. Ha ha.

The Fall - [June 2025]

The Backcast published its second issue. Due to reasons we cannot openly discuss, The Backcast was shut down and all copies ordered to be burned. We allegedly complied with this demand.

The Rebirth - [Summer Break 2025]

We were defeated. After a small party to celebrate the life & times of The Backcast, a wise man urged Hassan out of his cowardice; we unanimously decided to continue officially. Our revival was originally called 'The Outcast', but that sounded emo, lame, and honestly really boring. We decided on 'The Gadfly', though other names (Hamelin, Reynard, Errata, Persona Non Grata) were also floated around.

Early Gadfly - [September 2025]

With our heads held high, newspaper and paperwork in hand, we met with the principal as soon as possible. He let us continue, probably so that we wouldn't keep bugging him; now we have a functional, non-stalker relationship. Going forwards, we're official and follow all the rules. We soon published our first issue under The Gadfly name.

The Gadfly Today - [2025 Onwards)

In order to fill the customary fourth-member-who-doesn't-do-anything role, we expanded the team by 33.3333333334%. We continue to publish at our monthly cadence, much to the delight of our limited audience, and to the dismay of everyone else.
